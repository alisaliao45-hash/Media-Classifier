Media Ballot

About this project

Media Classifier is a data collection tool made to help train an AI model that can classify the medium most typically preferred by users for a given prompt. The idea is that certain mediums are ideal for certain types of content, and that a multimedia AI chatbot would be much more intuitive and helpful towards user comprehension as compared to today's text-based interfaces. This is the very first step in the process of building such a model, and is just an exploration made by me, a student. Your help is greatly appreciated!

What's the best medium for this prompt?

Think about what would be most helpful for the user's understanding of the answer, and what would feel most intuitive.
